Lasagna Recipe

A quick and easy recipe for making your own delicious lasagna

Our recipe makes a great lasagna but you should feel free to alter the ingredients to fit your needs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6.5 oz cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 6 oz cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tbsp of white sugar
  • 12 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 1 large egg
  • 1.5 tsp of dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp of fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp of Italian seasoning
  • 1.5 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp of parsley, chopped
  • 16 oz of ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 lb of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup of parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Cook the sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a dutch oven over a medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water and stir.
  3. Add the sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, pepper, 2 tablespoons of parsley and 1 teaspoon of salt. Stir well, cover and simmer for 1.5 hours, stir occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles in for 8-10 minutes. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. Combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 2 tablespoons of parsley and 0.5 teaspoons of salt in a mixing bowl.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  7. Layer the meat sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese mixture and other cheeses in a baking dish. Do so in the following order:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan
  8. Bake in the oven for 50 minutes, rest for 15 minutes before serving.
