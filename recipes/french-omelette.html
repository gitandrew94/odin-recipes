French Omelette Recipe

A traditional french omelette.

The french omelette requires few ingredients but plenty of technique to perfect.

Ingredients:

  • 3 large fresh eggs
  • 1/2 tsp cold water
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 pinch of cayenne or white pepper, to taste (optional)

Directions:

  1. Whisk the eggs, water and salt together until very liquid and whites are completely blended in
  2. Heat 1.5 tablespoons of butter in a 9-10 inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. As soon as the butter fully melts, pour in the whisked eggs.
  4. Stir in a circular pattern, lifting and scrambling the eggs & shaking the pan to keep levelling out the mixture. Stop once the eggs no longer level out.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and use a spatula to move runny eggs to less runny spots, aiming for an even thickness.
  6. As soon as the surface is wet but not runny, remove from the heat.
  7. Use the spatula to roll the omelette into a cylinder. Use the spatula to fold the last flat of egg over the top, leaving seam side up.
  8. Add the remaining butter to the pan, gently push melted butter under the omelette.
  9. Dust with cayenne or white pepper.
