Egg Fried Rice Recipe

A recipe for egg fried rice using simple ingredients.

This recipe has minimal ingredients to keep prep and cooking simple, a great starting place for amateur cooks.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of uncooked instant rice
  • 1 tsp of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup green beans
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper

Directions:

  1. Add the salt to the water and bring it to a boil
  2. Stir in the rice, remove from the heat and let it stand for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat the oil in a skillet/wok over a medium heat.
  4. Saut√© the onions and green beans for 2-3 minutes
  5. Pour in the beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling the egg as it cooks
  6. Combine the cooked rice with the egg mixture, season with the pepper.
